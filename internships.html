Want to be a part of building an exciting Software product? Considering an internship?

Pursuits, a Fortes Consulting venture is offering several internships with opportunities to learn within an early-stage product start-up team situated at the Chicago Innovation Exchange (University of Chicago).

Oppportunities

The internships for the current period (Jan - May 2015) are in the following:

1. Visual Design (branding, identity, etc.)
2. Front-end Programming (HTML5, JQuery, AngularJS, etc.)
3. Sales and Marketing (Market research, new market development, go-to-market strategy, etc.)
4. Machine Learning
5. Back-end Programming (.NET, C#, ASP.NET, RESTful API, etc.)

We love to mentor students in the above fields, aligned with their studies at respective schools. There are opportunties to learn more, especially building products within a startup environment.

We are working with career advancement and enterpreneurship offices of several Chicago-area schools (including University of Chicago and DePaul), so that you can earn credits and/or stipend/pay for the internship.

Please ask your school to get in touch with us, or email webcontact@fortes-consulting.com for details.
